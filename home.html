about us

We began our restaurant in the late 1990s because we wanted to create an authentic taste of Pakistan and maintain a connection to our homeland.
Khan Kabab House was born to fulfill that need.

We offer catering for all types of events: weddings, celebrations, birthdays and anniversaries, and other gatherings like team meetings and corporate events.
